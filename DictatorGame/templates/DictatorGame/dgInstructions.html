Instructions

In this task you are matched with one other person.

One of you will be Person A, one of you will be Person B.

Person A starts with 100 points, and Person B starts with 0 points.

This task has one single decision: Person A will choose how many of the 100 points to transfer to Person B.

Person B will get the number of points Person A transfers, and Person A will get to keep the rest.

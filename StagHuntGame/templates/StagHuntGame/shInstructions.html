Instructions

In this task you are matched with three other people.

All of you start with 100 points.

Each of you will choose whether to contribute 30 points to a group project.

All individuals decide at the same time.

If all people contribute, the group project is successful. In this case, all contributed points will be doubled and split evenly among the group.

If only one, two, or three people contribute, the group project is not successful, and all the points in it are lost.

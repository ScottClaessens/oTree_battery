Instructions

In this task you are matched with one other person.

One of you will be Person A, one of you will be Person B.

Both individuals start with 100 points.

This task has two phases. In each phase, both people choose at the same time.

Phase 1:

  1. First, both people will choose whether to transfer 30 of their points to a group project.
    • If both people transfer 30 points, then the group project is successful. This means that the 60 points in the group project will be doubled and distributed evenly between both players. Each person will end the phase with 130 points (100 - 30 transferred + 60 received).
    • If only one person transfers 30 points, then the group project is not successful. In this case, the person transferring points will lose those 30 points. The other person gets to keep their 100 points.
    • If neither person transfers 30 points, then they simply keep the 100 points they had to start with.

Phase 2:

  1. Based on the other person's decision in Phase 1, each person then chooses whether they want to remove up to 50 points from the other person. For every 5 points someone wants to remove from the other person, they must pay 1 point.

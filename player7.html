Payoffs

We have successfully matched you to other participants. In the rare event that we could not find a participant to match you with, we have instead matched you with average decisions based on previous research.

For each task, we randomly assigned you to a role (if there were multiple roles) and determined the payoffs for you and the participant(s) you were matched to.

Below are the results of each of the tasks.


Task 1

In this task, all four people started with 100 points.

You chose not to contribute 30 points to the group project.

Overall, only 3 group members contributed, and so the group project was unsuccessful. All points in it were lost.

Your payoff for this task was 100 points.


Task 2

In this task, both people started with 100 points.

In Phase 1, both people chose not to transfer 30 points to the group project. At the end of Phase 1, therefore, your payoff was 100 points.

In Phase 2, you removed 48 points from the other person for their choice in Phase 1. This cost you 10 points.

The other person removed 50 points from you.

Your payoff from both phases of this task was 40 points.


Task 3

In this task, you were assigned the role of Person A. Both people started with 50 points.

You transferred your 50 points to Person B, which was multiplied to 150 points. Person B then sent back 19 points.

Your payoff for this task was 19 points.


Task 4

In this task, all four people started with 100 points.

You contributed 32 points to the group project. The other group members contributed 4 points, 93 points, and 9 points. The total number of points was doubled and divided evenly between all people. This was added to your remaining points.

Your payoff for this task was 137 points.


Task 5

In this task, you were assigned the role of Person B. Person A started with 100 points and you started with nothing.

Person A offered you 66 points. Your minimum acceptable offer of 21 points was below this, and so you accepted their offer.

Your payoff for this task was 66 points.


Task 6

In this task, you were assigned the role of Person A. You started with 100 points and Person B started with nothing.

You transferred 62 points to Person B.

Your payoff for this task was 38 points.


Task 7

In this task, you were assigned the role of Person C. All people started with 100 points.

Person A chose to take points from Person B. Person B lost 50 points and Person A gained 30 points.

As a result, you then removed 79 points from Person A. This cost you 16 points.

Your payoff for this task was 84 points.


Your overall payoff from all tasks was 568 points. This means that your bonus for the study is $17.04.

If for some reason, your bonus for the study ended up less than $10, then we have rounded it up to $10.

PLEASE READ THESE INSTRUCTIONS CAREFULLY!

Thank you for participating in our study.

In this study, you will be asked to take part in a series of tasks. In each task you will be matched with one or more other people. You will be re-matched randomly from one task to the next, so the chance of you interacting with the same person in consecutive tasks is very small. You will never learn the identity of the people you are interacting with. They will never learn your identity. The money that you are reimbursed from these tasks depends on the decisions that you and the other participants make in the different tasks.

The payoffs for each interaction will be given in points. Every point will be worth $0.05 at the end of the study. For example, an interaction for 100 points has $5.00 at stake. The rules for each interaction will be different, so please read the instructions carefully!

Some interactions will have different "roles" for different participants. We will ask you what you would like to do in each possible role.

Every interaction will count towards your final payoff. At the end of the study, after matching your decisions with those of other random participants, we will calculate what you earned in each interaction. The sum of total of all these earnings will determine your bonus payment from the study.

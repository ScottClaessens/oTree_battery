Instructions

In this task you are matched with two other people.

One of you will be Person A, one of you will be Person B, one of you will be Person C.

All individuals start with 100 points.

First, Person A makes a choice, then Person C responds.

  1. Person A can choose to take or not take from Person B.
  2. If A takes, Person B loses 50 points and A gains 30 points.
  3. If A takes, then Person C can choose to remove points from A. Person C can remove up to 100 points from A.

Person C must pay 1 point for every 5 points they remove from A. Person B is passive in this task and makes no decisions.

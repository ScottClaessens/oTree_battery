Instructions

In this task you are matched with one other person.

One of you will be Person A, one of you will be Person B.

Both individuals start with 100 points.

This task has two phases. In each phase, both people choose at the same time.

Phase 1:

  1. First, both people will choose to transfer 30 of their points to the other person or not. Any points are doubled and given to the other person.

Phase 2:

  1. Based on the other person's decision in Phase 1, each person then chooses whether they want to remove up to 50 points from the other person. For every 5 points someone wants to remove from the other person, they must pay 1 point.

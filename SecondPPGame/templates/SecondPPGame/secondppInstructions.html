Instructions

In this interaction you are matched with one other brand new person.

One of you will be person A, one of you will be person B.

All individuals start with 100 points.

This interaction has two parts, in each part both people choose at the same time.

The transfer phase:

  1. First, both people will choose to transfer 30 of their points to the other person or not. Any points are doubled and given to the other person.

The penalty phase:

  1. Based on the other person's transfer decision, each person then chooses whether they want to remove up to 70 points from that other person. For every 5 points someone wants to remove from the other person, they must pay 1 point.

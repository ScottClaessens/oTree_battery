Instructions

In this task you are matched with one other person.

One of you will be Person A, one of you will be Person B.

Both individuals start with 100 points.

This task has two phases. In each phase, both people choose at the same time.

The transfer phase

In the transfer phase, both people will choose to transfer 30 of their points to the other person or not. Any points transferred are doubled and given to the other person.

The penalty phase

In the penalty phase, each person then chooses whether they want to remove up to 50 points from the other person, based on the other person's decision in the transfer phase. For every 5 points someone wants to remove from the other person, they must pay 1 point.

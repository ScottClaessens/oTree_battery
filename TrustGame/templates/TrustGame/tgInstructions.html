Instructions

In this task you are matched with one other person.

One of you will be Person A, the other will be Person B.

Both of you start with 50 points.

First, Person A makes a choice, then Person B responds.

  1. Person A can choose to transfer their 50 points or not. If Person A transfers 50 points then it is TRIPLED and given to Person B (so Person B now has 200 points; the 50 points sent by Person A is tripled to 150 points, which is added to the 50 points Person B had to begin with).
  2. Person B can then choose how many of the points they want to transfer back to person A (between 0 and 150). This latter amount is not tripled.
